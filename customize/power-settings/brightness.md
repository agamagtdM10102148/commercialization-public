# Brightness


Specifies the percentage value to scale brightness to when Energy Saver is turned on.

-   **Windows Provisioning:** `Brightness`

-   **PowerCfg:** `ESBRIGHTNESS`

-   **GUID:** 13d09884-f74e-474a-a852-b6bde8ad03a8

-   **Hidden setting:** Yes
